# 12 镜头 TapNow Prompts

> 直接放进 TapNow 图生视频节点。每条先生成 5–8 秒版本，剪辑时再拼接。
> 每条都已含风格尾巴。**Negative Prompt 见文末，每条都加。**
> 节点结构：`角色参考图 + 场景参考图 + 风格圣经 → 静态关键帧 → Image to Video / 首尾帧生视频 → 挑选最佳 → 导出`

---

## 镜头 1：闹钟跳时

```
The cute round fluffy creature sleeps in a small childlike bedroom. The alarm clock suddenly glitches, jumping from 7:00 to 8:30. The fluffy creature rolls off the bed and lands softly on the floor, looking confused and unlucky. Tiny hand-drawn time glitch lines appear around the clock. Slow funny movement, gentle camera shake. childlike hand-drawn animation style, crayon texture, soft watercolor colors, imperfect lines, warm urban fairy tale mood, 2D animation, no photorealism, no logos
```

---

## 镜头 2：城市时间卡顿

```
A simple childlike city morning. A traffic light is stuck on red, a bus stop screen keeps flashing "1 minute", and a classroom wall clock freezes on the same second. The round fluffy creature looks around in confusion. The city feels ordinary but slightly broken, with small hand-drawn time glitch ripples. childlike hand-drawn animation style, crayon texture, soft watercolor colors, 2D animation, urban glitch fairy tale mood, no real brand logos
```

> 一个镜头装不下三个场景时，拆成三个短镜头：
> - `traffic light stuck on red`
> - `bus stop screen keeps flashing "1 minute"`
> - `classroom wall clock freezes and repeats the same second`

## 镜头 9：毛球变女孩（首尾帧生视频）

- 首帧：小毛球闭眼，被光包围。
- 尾帧：白衣黄裙小女孩站在原地。

```
The round fluffy creature is wrapped in gentle golden-white light. Its round body transforms softly, not scary, like a child's dream. The skipped time becomes warm glowing memories around it. When the light fades, a little girl with long hair, a simple white top, and a yellow skirt stands in the same place. She looks down at her hands with wonder and a small smile. childlike hand-drawn animation style, crayon texture, soft watercolor colors, magical but gentle, 2D animation
```

> 备用方案（首尾帧翻车）：① 毛球被光包围 → ② 闪白 → ③ 女孩站原地 → ④ 旁白"我好像长大了"。
